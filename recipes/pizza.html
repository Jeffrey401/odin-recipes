Pizza

Description

Crispy crust, with melted cheese, red tomato sauce, topped with crunchy pepperoni

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Chedder and Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Roll out dough into a circular shape to your desired thickness
  2. Spread tomato sauce over the dough, 1 inch away from the edge
  3. Top with cheese
  4. Top with pepperoni
  5. Bake in oven at 400 degrees for 25 minutes
