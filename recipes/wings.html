Wings

Description

Crispy fried chicken wings

Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings
  • Flour
  • Adobo Seasoning
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Dry chicken wings
  2. Toss all seasonings in bowl with chicken and mix
  3. Marinate for 20 minutes
  4. Lightly coat in flour
  5. Put in the air fryer for 2 minutes
