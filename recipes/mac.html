Baked Mac and Cheese

Description

Pasta, gooey cheese sauce, crispy crust

Ingredients

  • Elbow Pasta Shells
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Milk
  • Sour Cream
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt
  • Paprika
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil Shells
  2. Make cheese sauce
  3. Put shells in a tray
  4. Dump cheese sauce over shells and mix
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes
  6. Broil for 5 minutes for crust
  7. Let cool and enjoy
